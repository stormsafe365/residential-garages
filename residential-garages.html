Engineered for Florida Weather

Florida's climate punishes weak construction. Between hurricane-force winds, relentless humidity, salt air, and intense UV exposure — your garage needs to be engineered for survival, not just shelter.

  • Engineered to meet local wind load requirements up to 170+ MPH
  • Galvanized steel resists rust, corrosion, and salt air damage
  • Designed for hurricane-prone regions across FL & the Southeast
  • Prepared with stamped engineered plans for permitting
  • No wood framing means no termite risk and no rot
  • Fire-resistant construction for added safety
